About me

I am driven by learning and by the opportunity to solve meaningful problems in healthcare.

During a night shift in the Emergency & Trauma Department, arranging transport for one patient required three separate web applications. While other patients waited, I thought: there must be a better way to design this process. That experience turned my interest in technology into a clear goal—to bring nursing knowledge and health informatics together and create digital tools that genuinely support the people delivering care.
